# QBR Templates Reference

Standard templates for QBR content generation.

## Deck Structure by Segment

### Enterprise QBR (60-90 minutes)

10-15 slides. Heavy on data, ROI, and strategic alignment.
1. Title and agenda
2. Executive summary (1 slide, 3-4 bullet points max)
3. Relationship overview (key milestones since last QBR)
4. Usage and adoption (2-3 slides with charts)
5. Value delivered (ROI metrics, outcomes)
6. Support summary
7. Product roadmap alignment
8. Strategic recommendations (CSM-written)
9. Discussion / open items
10. Action items and next steps

### Mid-Market QBR (45-60 minutes)

8-10 slides. Balanced between data and conversation.
1. Title and agenda
2. Usage and adoption (1-2 slides)
3. Value delivered (key metrics)
4. Support summary (brief)
5. Looking ahead (product updates, adoption opportunities)
6. Discussion
7. Action items

### SMB QBR (30 minutes, may be email-based)

5-6 slides or a formatted email. Focus on quick wins and next steps.
1. Usage snapshot
2. Value highlights
3. Tips and recommendations
4. What is coming next
5. Action items

## Follow-Up Email Template

**Structure:**
1. Thank attendees for their time
2. Key takeaways (3-5 bullet points)
3. Action items with owners and deadlines
4. Next QBR target date
5. Open invitation for questions between QBRs
