# Segment Variations

## Enterprise (8-12 weeks)
Formal phases, multi-stakeholder, custom configuration, weekly touchpoints during activation.

## Mid-Market (4-6 weeks)
Standard phases, 2-3 customer contacts, guided self-configuration, check-ins at day 7/14/30.

## SMB (1-3 weeks)
Compressed, mostly self-serve with optional office hours, automated email sequence, template-based.
