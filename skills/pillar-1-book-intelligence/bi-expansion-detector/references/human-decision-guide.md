# Human Decision Guide: Expansion Signals

## Evaluation Framework

1. **Validate the signal** -- does it match your relationship read?
2. **Assess timing** -- is the customer in a position to buy? Budget, priorities, relationship health
3. **Frame the approach** -- expansion should feel like a natural extension of the value conversation
4. **Decide** -- pursue now, nurture first, park, or override the health filter if you know better

## Framing by Expansion Type

- **Seat growth**: "Your team has outgrown the current setup -- let's make sure everyone has access"
- **Tier upgrade**: "I noticed your team hitting the limits of [feature]. The next tier unlocks [specific capability]"
- **Cross-sell**: "Based on how [current team] uses us, [new product] could extend that value to [new team]"
