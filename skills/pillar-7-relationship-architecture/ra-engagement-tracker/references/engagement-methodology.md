# Engagement Methodology

## Touchpoint Channels
Email (sent/received), calendar (meetings), calls (completed), support (ticket interactions), product (portal logins -- passive engagement).

## Engagement Health Levels
- Strong: all key contacts active, trend stable or growing, multi-channel
- Adequate: most contacts active, some cooling, stable trend
- Declining: trend down, 1+ key contact in Gap status
- Critical: multiple contacts Gap/Lost, trend declining, single-channel

## Expected Cadence by Stakeholder Type
- Champion: 2 weeks (enterprise), monthly (mid-market)
- Economic Buyer: monthly (enterprise), quarterly (mid-market)
- Executive Sponsor: quarterly
