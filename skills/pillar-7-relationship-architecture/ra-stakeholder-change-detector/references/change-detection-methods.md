# Change Detection Methods

## CRM-Based (High Confidence)
Contact deactivated, role changed, new contact added, contact reassigned. Triggers on CRM field change events.

## Engagement-Based (Medium Confidence)
Sudden silence (30+ days no activity from previously active contact), email bounce, meeting delegate pattern, new voice emerging. Behavioural inference -- multiple explanations possible.

## External (Variable Confidence)
LinkedIn role/company change, new hire at customer with relevant title. Depends on integration availability and data freshness.
