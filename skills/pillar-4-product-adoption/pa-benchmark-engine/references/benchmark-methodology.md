# Benchmark Methodology

## Cohort Definition
Accounts grouped by: product tier, company size band, contract age cohort. Minimum 20 accounts per cohort for reliable benchmarks.

## Percentile Computation
Standard percentile calculation across the cohort for each metric. Compute p5, p25, p50, p75, p95.

## Refresh Cadence
Quarterly full refresh. Interim updates when cohort composition changes significantly (large batch of new customers).

## Customer-Safe Output
Anonymise all comparisons. Present as "companies similar to yours." Never expose individual account data.
