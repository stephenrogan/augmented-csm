# Deck Design Guidelines

## Data Visualisation
- One key metric per chart. Do not overload slides
- Use consistent colour coding across all slides
- Label axes and include time periods
- Show trend direction with arrows or colour (green improving, red declining)

## Slide Principles
- No slide should take more than 30 seconds to read
- Every data slide needs a "so what" headline (not just a chart title)
- Strategic recommendation slides are CSM territory -- leave as structured placeholders
- Customer-appropriate language only. No internal jargon, risk scores, or CS terminology
