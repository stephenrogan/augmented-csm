# Email Template Library

## Template Principles

- Every email has one purpose. If you need to cover two topics, send two emails
- Subject lines are specific: "Follow-up: API performance discussion" not "Checking in"
- First sentence states the purpose. No throat-clearing
- Last sentence is the action item or question
- Under 200 words for routine comms
- Never start with "I hope this email finds you well"

## Check-In Email

Subject: Quick check-in -- {topic}

Hi {first_name},

{One sentence acknowledging recent context.}

{One specific question or observation.}

{One clear next step.}

Best,
{CSM name}

## Post-Call Follow-Up

Subject: Follow-up: {topic discussed}

Hi {first_name},

Thanks for the time today. Quick summary:

{2-4 bullet points}

Action items:
- {Action 1} -- {owner} by {date}
- {Action 2} -- {owner} by {date}

{Next touchpoint.}

Best,
{CSM name}

## Tone Calibration

| Segment | Tone | Formality |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Enterprise | Professional, measured | High |
| Mid-Market | Warm professional | Medium |
| SMB | Casual, direct | Low |
