# Extraction Patterns

## Commitment Language Patterns

### High Confidence
- "I will [action] by [date]"
- "We will [action]"
- "Let me [action] -- I will have it to you by [date]"
- "Consider it done"
- "You will have that by [date]"

### Medium Confidence
- "Let's [action]" (owner ambiguous -- flag for confirmation)
- "We should [action]" (intent without commitment)
- "I can look into that" (offer without timeline)
- "We will get back to you" (commitment without specificity)

### Low Confidence
- "That is a good idea" (agreement without action)
- "We will think about that" (deferral, not commitment)
- "Maybe we could..." (speculative)

## Sentiment Indicators

### Positive
Explicit: "love", "great", "impressed", "happy with", "exactly what we needed"
Implicit: Forward-looking language, expansion mentions, voluntarily sharing internal plans

### Negative
Explicit: "frustrated", "disappointed", "concerned", "not what we expected"
Implicit: Repeated questions on the same topic, hedging language, short answers, delegation to a junior person

### Competitive
Explicit: Naming a competitor, asking about feature comparisons
Implicit: "Other options", "what the market offers", "we have been looking at alternatives"
