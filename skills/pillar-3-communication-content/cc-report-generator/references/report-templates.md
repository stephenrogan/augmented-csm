# Report Templates

## CSM Weekly Report
1. Portfolio health summary (accounts by band, week-over-week changes)
2. This week's priorities (upcoming touchpoints, overdue items)
3. Risk queue (accounts needing attention, sorted by urgency)
4. Expansion pipeline (active signals with estimated value)
5. Key metrics: accounts touched, health trend, commitment completion rate
Distribution: Individual CSM. Delivered Monday morning.

## Manager Monthly Report
1. Team portfolio health (aggregate and per-CSM breakdown)
2. Renewal pipeline (next 90 days by risk classification)
3. Churn and expansion (trailing 30 days)
4. Process metrics: touchpoint compliance, QBR completion, SLA adherence
5. Capacity: accounts per CSM, health distribution per CSM
Distribution: CS manager. Delivered first business day of the month.

## Leadership Quarterly Report
1. NRR by segment (trailing 12 months and current quarter)
2. Churn analysis with root causes
3. Expansion revenue and pipeline
4. Health trajectory across the portfolio
5. Segment-level trends and outliers
6. Strategic recommendations (human-written section)
Distribution: VP CS, CRO, CFO. Delivered within 5 business days of quarter close.
