# Brief Templates

## Escalation Brief
1. Headline (one line: what, who, impact)
2. Timeline (chronological list of events)
3. Customer impact (business effect, not just technical description)
4. Resolution attempts (what was tried, what was the outcome)
5. Specific ask (what you need, by when)
6. Customer communication status (what they know, what they expect)
7. Commercial context (ARR, renewal date, risk level)

## Leadership Summary
1. One-line status
2. ARR, health score, renewal date
3. Primary risk or opportunity
4. Recommended executive action (if any)
Length: 5-8 lines maximum.

## Cross-Functional Context Brief
1. Account overview (tailored to the audience)
2. Relevant history (only what this team needs)
3. Specific ask with deadline
4. Success criteria (how you will know the ask is met)
