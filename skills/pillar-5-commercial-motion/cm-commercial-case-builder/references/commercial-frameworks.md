# Commercial Case Frameworks

## Internal Prep Document
Full evidence package, pricing options, discount authority, risk factors, negotiation notes.

## Customer-Facing Summary
Value delivered so far (lead with gains), growth evidence (natural progression), what expansion unlocks (specific capabilities), investment overview (pricing context, not formal quote).

## Framing by Expansion Type
- Seat growth: capacity constraint evidence + organic demand
- Tier upgrade: feature-gating hits + power user evidence
- Cross-sell: new team adoption + adjacent use case evidence
