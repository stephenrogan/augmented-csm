# Competitive Response Frameworks

## By Evaluation Stage

### Early Consideration
Proactive value reinforcement. Do not mention the competitor. Lead with "I want to make sure we are covering everything you need."

### Active Evaluation
Address the underlying need. "I understand you are looking at how to solve [problem]. Let me show you how we can address that."

### Decision Imminent
Direct engagement. Executive involvement if warranted. Commercial flexibility if authorised. Focus on risk of switching vs. risk of staying.
