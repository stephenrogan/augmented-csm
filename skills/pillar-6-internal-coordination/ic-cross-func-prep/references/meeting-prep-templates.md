# Meeting Prep Templates

## Manager 1:1
Focus: CSM's portfolio, specific accounts, development
Data: Health distribution, risk queue, expansion pipeline, cadence compliance, action items from last 1:1
Format: 1-page summary + account briefs for 3-5 discussion accounts

## Pipeline Review
Focus: Revenue outlook, risk, expansion
Data: Renewal forecast with scenarios, at-risk accounts with intervention status, expansion pipeline, recent churn
Format: Dashboard view with drill-down available per account. Decisions flagged

## CS-Product Sync
Focus: Customer voice, adoption, product feedback
Data: Top feature request themes, adoption blockers, product-correlated churn, upcoming release impact
Format: Ranked list with ARR weight and customer evidence. Roadmap status per theme

## Escalation Committee
Focus: Blocked issues, resource needs
Data: Active escalations with age and owner, stalled items, resolution rate trends
Format: Status table sorted by severity and age
