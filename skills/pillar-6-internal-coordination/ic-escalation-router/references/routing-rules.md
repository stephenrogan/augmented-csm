# Escalation Routing Rules

| Category | Routes To | Typical Trigger |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Product issue (bug) | Engineering via support | P1/P2 not resolved within SLA |
| Feature gap | Product management | Customer blocked by missing capability |
| Service failure | CS + support leadership | SLA breach, repeated failures |
| Commercial dispute | CS leadership + finance | Billing issue, contract disagreement |
| Relationship at risk | CS leadership | Champion departure, competitive threat |
| Executive intervention | CRO or CEO office | Strategic account, existential churn risk |

## Acknowledgement SLA
Receiving team must acknowledge within 4 business hours. If no acknowledgement, re-route with escalation to team manager.
